About me

I am Tharshika Jeyakumar – an ambitious multimedia designer with a passion for graphic design, videography and photography.
My desire to meet people’s unique needs when it comes to content creating and graphic design led me to study Multimedia design in Odense.
My personal aim is to creatively combine a pragmatic approach in an aesthetic way. I love to communicate through videography while simultaneously improving communication between people around the world. My specialty is a combination of photography, videography and graphic design combined in an aesthetically pleasing way.
Besides my interest in graphic design, videography and photography, I have experience with HTML5 and CSS3, as well as some experience in Atom and Visual Studio Code, Wordpress and the entire Adobe package.
