A Day In My Life

Year: 2020

Client: School Project

Task: Videpgrapher, concept maker and editor

Role: Photographer & Editor

Info:

During my first semester at UCL Erhvervsakademi og Professionhøjskole, Odense, we had a school assignment, where we could either choose to make a video of ‘A day in our lives’ or choose an already existing trailer and change the genre. I chose the A day in my life, because with this specific assignment I had the chance to film clips from angles I liked. I decided to film my 20th birthday from my own perspective, which is why the concept with this video is to take the audience with me on a day in my life from my perspective. Normally I would film this with a camera, but this time I filmed with my phone. This was my final result.
