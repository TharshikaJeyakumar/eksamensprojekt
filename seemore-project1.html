Year: 2020

Client: School Project

Task: Graphic design & UX design

Role: Graphic designer & UX Testing

Info:

During my first semester at UCL Erhvervsakademi og Professionhøjskole, Odense, our first project was to make a redesign of an already existing website. The clients liked the new look of the website and are considering using the design.
