Project 2

Year: 2020

Client: School Project

Task: Installation Development

Role: Content Creator

Info:

Our second project during the first semester at UCL Erhvervsakademi og Professionhøjskole, Odense, was to make a multimedia installation for a client. The client’s main concerns was that they have reduced scope due to the fact that they have only been able to appeal to professionals.
Our solution to the client was based on the lack of an interactive aspect in ‘Bylaboratoriet’s exhibition. Moreover, the current exhibitions lack character which the younger generation are naturally attracted towards in this day and time. Hence why, our solution would be able to not only attract to the younger generation, but also retain their interest, which is key to the success of this company as it will widen the target audience and thus automatically increase its popularity and profit. Our solution to the given problem is based on simple technology, which makes the installation more effective and therefore has reduced maintenance costs.
